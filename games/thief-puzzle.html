🎯 About This Game

Master the art of sneaky theft in this engaging puzzle adventure that combines humor with brain-teasing challenges. Control a cunning thief equipped with an extraordinary stretchy arm capable of reaching incredible distances - even wrapping around the Statue of Liberty!

Navigate through 400 unique levels of cartoon-style heist scenarios where every grab requires careful planning. From snatching diamond rings to pilfering grocery bags, each mission presents a new comedic challenge filled with traps, obstacles, and unexpected chain reactions.

✨ Key Features

  • 🤏 Elastic Arm Mechanics - Stretch your character's arm to incredible lengths and reach impossible distances
  • 🎯 400 Unique Levels - Each stage offers a distinct story and puzzle challenge with progressive difficulty
  • 🎨 Cartoon Animation Style - Enjoy stickman graphics with humorous visual effects and comedic scenarios
  • 📖 Story-Driven Gameplay - Experience mini-narratives in every level with cross-platform compatibility
  • 🧩 Strategic Puzzles - Plan your heist routes around obstacles and security systems
  • 🎮 Cross-Platform - Play seamlessly on desktop and mobile devices

🕹️ Game Controls

🖱️ Desktop Controls

  • 🎯 Click & Drag - Extend the thief's arm toward your target
  • 🧭 Navigate Obstacles - Adjust drag path to avoid traps
  • 🎪 Strategic Planning - Observe environment before acting

📱 Mobile Controls

  • 👆 Touch & Drag - Control arm movement anywhere on screen
  • 🎯 Grab Target - Release when positioned over desired object
  • 🔄 Retry Levels - Learn from failure animations

🌍 Adventure Scenarios

🗼 Paris Adventure

Navigate the romantic streets of Paris where you must acquire a mysterious suitcase near the Eiffel Tower. Dodge fire hydrants, avoid tourists taking selfies, and watch out for floating red balloons.

💡 Strategy Tips

Observe your environment carefully and identify security traps, alarm systems, and hostile characters. Plan your route around obstacles and time-sensitive elements. Don't be afraid to experiment - failure animations provide both humor and strategic insight for your next approach!
